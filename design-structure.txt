# 🚆 BGS Sidoarjo — Landing Page Design Structure (Modern Minimalist, Light Mode)

## 🧠 Core Principle

* Clean, modern, human-centered
* Fokus ke **komunitas & impact nyata**
* Visual harus terasa **ringan, rapi, dan profesional**
* **Photo kegiatan > elemen dekoratif**

---

## 📐 Global Layout Rules

* Max width: `1200px`
* Section padding: `80px – 120px`
* Grid: 12-column system
* Spacing antar section: besar (biar lega)
* Background:

  * putih dominan
  * selingan light gray (`#F9FAFB`)

---

## 🧱 PAGE STRUCTURE

---

# 1. NAVBAR

### Layout:

* Horizontal (fixed top)
* Height: ~72px

### Content:

* Left: Logo / Text → **BGS Sidoarjo**
* Center: Navigation

  * Tentang
  * Kegiatan
  * Galeri
* Right:

  * Button: **Join Komunitas**

### Style:

* Background: transparan → jadi putih saat scroll
* Border bottom tipis saat scroll
* Button:

  * warna kuning (primary)

---

# 2. HERO SECTION

### Layout:

* 2 column (desktop):

  * kiri: text
  * kanan: image
* Mobile: stacked

### Content:

* Title:
  → **BGS Sidoarjo**
* Subtitle:
  → komunitas railfans fokus edukasi, keselamatan, dan kolaborasi
* Badge kecil:
  → “Diakui oleh PT KAI”
* CTA:

  * Primary: Join Komunitas (kuning)
  * Secondary: Lihat Galeri (outline)

### Visual:

* Foto kereta / kegiatan (real photo)
* Clean, tidak rame

### Spacing:

* Top: besar (biar impactful)

---

# 3. TRUST / CREDIBILITY STRIP

### Layout:

* 3 column (desktop)

### Content:

* Diakui PT KAI
* Aktif Relawan Posko
* Sosialisasi Keselamatan

### Style:

* Background: light gray
* Icon kecil + text pendek
* Centered

---

# 4. ABOUT SECTION (Tentang BGS)

### Layout:

* 2 column:

  * kiri: text
  * kanan: image / ilustrasi

### Content:

* Judul: Tentang BGS
* Paragraf pendek:

  * komunitas railfans
  * fokus edukasi & sosial
* Bullet points:

  * edukasi
  * keselamatan
  * komunitas aktif

### Style:

* Typography clean
* Banyak whitespace

---

# 5. ACTIVITIES SECTION (Kegiatan)

### Layout:

* Grid 4 card (desktop)
* 2 kolom (tablet)
* 1 kolom (mobile)

### Cards:

1. Relawan Posko Stasiun
2. Sosialisasi Perlintasan KA
3. Train Spotting
4. Diskusi & Edukasi

### Card Style:

* Background: putih
* Border: tipis
* Shadow: soft
* Radius: 10px

### Interaction:

* Hover:

  * sedikit naik
  * shadow lebih jelas

---

# 6. GALLERY SECTION

### Layout:

* Grid:

  * 3–4 kolom desktop
  * gap konsisten

### Content:

* Foto kegiatan real
* Fokus ke:

  * posko
  * sosialisasi
  * spotting

### Interaction:

* Hover:

  * zoom dikit
  * overlay tipis

---

# 7. IMPACT / STATS SECTION

### Layout:

* 3 column centered

### Content:

* Anggota
* Kegiatan
* Dokumentasi

### Style:

* Angka besar (highlight)
* Label kecil di bawah
* Minimalist (no card berat)

---

# 8. CTA SECTION

### Layout:

* Centered

### Content:

* Headline:
  → “Gabung bareng BGS Sidoarjo sekarang”
* Subtext pendek
* Button:

  * Telegram
  * Discord

### Style:

* Background:

  * putih / sedikit aksen kuning lembut
* Fokus ke conversion

---

# 9. FOOTER

### Layout:

* 3 column

### Content:

* Logo + deskripsi singkat
* Navigasi
* Sosial media

### Style:

* Minimal
* Text kecil
* Background putih / abu tipis

---

## 🎨 DESIGN SYSTEM (SIMPLE)

### Colors:

* White: #FFFFFF
* Text: #111111
* Yellow: #FACC15
* Red: #E11D48
* Gray light: #F9FAFB
* Border: #E5E7EB

---

## 🔤 Typography:

* Heading: bold, clean sans-serif
* Body: regular, readable
* Spacing antar text lega

---

## ✨ Interaction Rules

* Hover: subtle only
* Animasi:

  * fade-in
  * slide-up ringan
* Tidak berlebihan

---

## 🚀 FINAL GOAL

Website harus terasa:

* bersih & modern
* komunitas yang aktif & nyata
* terpercaya (PT KAI)
* bikin orang:
  → “ini komunitas legit, gue pengen join”
